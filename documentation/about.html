Contact Form 7 - Insights Tracker is a monitoring tool for Contact Form 7 related activity. The main purpose is to provide a comprehensive overview of every form submission. It tracks the total count of submissions and displays the most recent submission data in a table format for easy viewing. This tool helps users keep track of form activity, analyze submission patterns, and ensure no form submissions are missed.

Features:

  • Easily track your contact form activity after installation with no additional inputs required. It just runs automatically after installation.
  • Analytics tab provides a complete list of all forms, total submission counts, and the last activity date for each form.
  • Supported languages: Tamil (ta), Italian (it), French (fr), Spanish (es), German (de), Chinese (zh).
  • Settings page contains various plugin-related details and customization options to suit your needs.
  • User-friendly interface that requires no technical knowledge to navigate and use effectively.
  • Real-time updates ensure that you always have the most current data about your form submissions.
  • Customizable notification settings to alert you of new submissions or any errors encountered.
  • Mobile-friendly design, ensuring you can monitor form submissions on the go.
  • Comprehensive documentation and support to assist with any issues or questions you might have.
  • Integration with other tools and plugins to enhance functionality and provide a seamless user experience.
  • Regular updates and improvements to ensure compatibility with the latest versions of WordPress and Contact Form 7.
  • Secure data handling to protect your submission data from unauthorized access.
  • Performance optimization to ensure your website remains fast and responsive even with high submission volumes.
  • Compatibility with various themes and plugins, ensuring it fits seamlessly into your existing WordPress setup.
