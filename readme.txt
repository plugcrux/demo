Track and analyze Contact Form 7 submissions to improve engagement and conversions.

== Description ==

The Form Insights Tracker plugin seamlessly integrates with Contact Form 7, one of the most popular form plugins for WordPress, to provide comprehensive performance tracking capabilities. This plugin allows you to monitor and analyze the performance of your Contact Form 7 forms by tracking the total number of form submissions and the date and time of the last submission.

=== Features ===

* **Automatic Tracking**: Track your contact form activity effortlessly after installation.
* **Analytics Tab**: View a complete list of all forms, total submission counts, and the last activity date for each form.
* **Multi-language Support**: Available in Tamil (ta), Italian (it), French (fr), Spanish (es), German (de), and Chinese (zh).
* **User-friendly Interface**: No technical knowledge required to navigate and use effectively.
* **Real-time Updates**: Get the most current data about your form submissions.
* **Customizable Notifications**: Set up alerts for new submissions or errors.
* **Mobile-friendly Design**: Access your reports on the go.

== Installation ==

1. Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard.
2. Navigate to the "Plugins" menu and click "Add New".
3. Search for "Form Insights Tracker".
4. Click "Install Now" and then "Activate".

== Usage ==

=== Viewing Reports ===

1. Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard.
2. Navigate to the "Contact" menu and click "Reports".

=== Understanding Performance Data ===

* **Form Name**: The title of the Contact Form 7 form.
* **Total Submissions**: The total number of submissions received for the form.
* **Last Record Added At**: The date and time of the last submission for the form.

=== Tracking Multiple Forms ===

The plugin tracks performance data for all Contact Form 7 forms on your WordPress site automatically after installation.
